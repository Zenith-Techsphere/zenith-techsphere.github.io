Android · Social

BookClub Social

Live
Trusted book lending for communities.

BookClub Social helps people share physical books within trusted communities. Users can add books manually or by scanning an ISBN barcode, create or join book clubs, lend books to others, manage borrow requests, verify handovers, chat during active transactions, and track lending history.

For communities, campuses, clubs, and local groups that share physical books.
Helps members catalog books, request borrows, verify handovers, chat, and track lending history.
Proof: listed on Google Play by Zenith Techsphere Pvt Ltd.

macOS · Utility

Prune

Live
Free your Mac from developer build waste, safely.

Prune is a native macOS app for finding and safely removing developer disk waste such as node_modules, Xcode DerivedData, Docker volumes, Homebrew caches, logs, and abandoned projects. The Gumroad product page lists scanning and manual cleanup as free, with Pro features for background cleaning, scheduled cleanups, and storage forecasting.

For Mac developers whose machines fill with build artifacts, dependency folders, logs, and caches.
Helps scan storage, review cleanup targets, and move selected items to Trash instead of hard-deleting them.
Proof: distributed as Prune Pro through Gumroad.
View Prune Status: Live on Gumroad

About

A registered Indian company building owned software assets

Zenith Techsphere Private Limited is registered in India and headquartered in Hyderabad. We build and operate software products that solve specific real-world problems.

We are not trying to look large. We are trying to become useful. Our focus is simple: build products, test them with users, improve them, and create durable software assets over time.

We prefer shipped products over pitch decks, practical use cases over vague innovation claims, and clear monetization over vanity traffic.
