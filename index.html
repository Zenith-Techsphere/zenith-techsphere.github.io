Android · Social

BookClub Social

Live
Community book sharing, borrowing, and lending.

BookClub Social helps people share physical books within trusted communities. Users can add books manually or by scanning an ISBN barcode, create or join book clubs, lend books to others, manage borrow requests, verify handovers, chat during active transactions, and track lending history.

Listed on Google Play by Zenith Techsphere Pvt Ltd.
Android package: com.zenithTechsphere.bookClubSocial.
Supports Google or phone sign-in, push notifications, and subscription limits for lending and groups.
View on Play Store Status: Live on Google Play
macOS · Utility

Prune

Live
A focused digital product available on Gumroad.

Prune is a native macOS app for finding and safely removing developer disk waste such as node_modules, Xcode DerivedData, Docker volumes, Homebrew caches, logs, and abandoned projects. The Gumroad product page lists scanning and manual cleanup as free, with Pro features for background cleaning, scheduled cleanups, and storage forecasting.

Distributed as Prune Pro through Gumroad.
The product page lists a one-time Pro purchase and no telemetry.
Cleanup moves items to Trash instead of hard-deleting them.
View Prune Status: Live on Gumroad

About
